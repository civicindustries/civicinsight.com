Company

Civic Insight makes official information about buildings and construction projects available to the public in an easy-to-use interface. We also develop visualizations that show how a city is changing over time.

Eddie Tejeda, Co-Founder and CEO

Eddie is Code for America Alum (2012), and previously co-founded a web consulting company and led independent, open-source projects that are currently used by universities, governments and libraries across the country. Eddie is engaged in his home city of Oakland, where he is the co-founder and Director of Technology of OpenOakland, and serves on the Public Ethics Commission for the City of Oakland overseeing government transparency.

Amir Reavis-Bey, Co-Founder and CTO

Amir is Code for America Alum (2012) who previously worked as an investment banking technologist in NYC, focused on equities trading and fixed-income business lines. With aspirations to repurpose his technology skills to better US cities, Amir served as a 2012 Code For America fellow, partnering with the city of New Orleans to develop applications focusing on vacancy and quality of life issues.
