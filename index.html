Knowledge is power.

BlightStatus provides universal and user-friendly access to official, real-time information about abandoned properties, making it easier for city staff and community groups to allocate resources, organize effectively, and make timely, strategic decisions to improve the quality and value of their communities.

The presence of just one blighted property on the average residential block can reduce the value of neighboring homes by up to $7,000 each.¹

For cities:

  • Interactive maps and charts help you identify patterns and make informed, strategic decisions.
  • Citizens now have answers at their fingertips, leaving you more time to focus on your work.
  • BlightStatus sits on top of your existing systems and workflows - no system overhaul required.

For citizens:

  • Browse a map or search by address to find out exactly what’s happening with a particular property.
  • Subscribe to properties you care about to receive timely email updates about their progress.
  • Get the answers you need, without the fuss of lengthy phone calls or trips to City Hall.
