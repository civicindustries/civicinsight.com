BlightStatus is now Civic Insight! Learn why here.

We’re harnessing the power of information to help rebuild our cities. We’re harnessing the power of information to help rebuild our cities.

Knowledge is power.

Civic Insight makes real-time government data about a community’s underutilized spaces – think abandoned homes, empty storefronts, or vacant lots – both publicly available and extremely easy to use and understand.


By getting everyone on the same page, Civic Insight makes it easier for government staff, motivated citizens, and local organizations to collaborate more effectively to improve the quality and value of their neighborhoods.

The presence of just one blighted property on the average residential block can reduce the value of neighboring homes by up to $7,000 each.¹

For cities:

  • Interactive maps and charts help you identify patterns and make informed, strategic decisions.
  • Citizens now have answers at their fingertips, leaving you more time to focus on your work.
  • Civic Insight sits on top of your existing systems and workflows - no system overhaul required.

For citizens:

  • Browse a map or search by address to find out exactly what’s happening with a particular property.
  • Subscribe to properties you care about to receive timely email updates about their progress.
  • Get the answers you need, without the fuss of lengthy phone calls or trips to City Hall.
