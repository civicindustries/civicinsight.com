Knowledge is power.

BlightStatus provides universal and user-friendly access to official, real-time information about abandoned properties, making it easier for city staff and resident-activists to allocate resources, organize effectively, and make timely, strategic decisions to improve the quality and value of their communities.

The presence of just one blighted property on the average residential block can reduce the value of neighboring homes by up to $7,000 each.¹

For cities:

  • BlightStatus provides answers to citizens’ questions, leaving you with more time to focus on moving properties through the blight abatement process.
  • Interactive maps and charts help you identify patterns and make informed, strategic decisions.
  • By providing universal and user-friendly access to official, real-time information about abandoned.

For citizens:

  • Find out exactly what’s going on with problem properties in your community, no long waits on the telephone or visits to City Hall required.
  • Add properties you care about to your Watchlist to receive timely email alerts about upcoming events like hearings, demolitions, etc.
  • By providing universal and user-friendly access to official, real-time information about abandoned.
